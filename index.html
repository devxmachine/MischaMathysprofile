Nobody wants to read a boring story

Nobody wants to read a boring story. That's an edict I've applied to all my work, which has ranged from extensive, in-depth articles for online media outlets to the type of prosaic information that occupies social media on a daily basis. I am a communications advisor with over 8 years experience in the music industry. I've worked extensively with various web media outlets, from niche blogs to universal social media channels with my experiences extending from the music industry to advertising. My credentials as a writer include, but are not exclusive to, Vice UK, DummyMag, Norient, Monument, Jaeger and Trouw Amsterdam. I've been published in both print and digital formats and work between freelance assignments and a fixed position at Iaeger Oslo. It was after working with Trouw-Amsterdam, which culminated in an assistant editor position for a book on the cultural institution, that I found my calling. Shortly after I moved to Oslo, Norway, where I've been working almost exclusively for Jaeger Oslo. Working closely alongside the owner, I have taken large strides in developing the company's website and blog, to maximise the UX and provide succinct and entertaining content for its followers. I continue to write on a freelance basis, taking on selective assignments from music magazines, labels and advertising agencies. I pride myself on the content I produce and stress quality over quantity in my work with a focus on creating engaging content, whether it be a published article or an internal document. I strive to employ strategies that will engage with our audiences on a personal level. My eye for detail is almost obsessive and I enjoy innovating wherever I can in a creative manner. With years of experience working as a freelancer, I'm structured and manage deadlines with precision. I enjoy learning new skills and expanding on my knowledge, keeping up to date with all the latest developments in music and it's various industries.
